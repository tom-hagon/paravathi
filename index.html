Prabavathi recepi for the best chicken gravy

Ingredients

  • 2 piece of cinnamon
  • few geera and sombu(it's upto our taste)
  • 1/2 coconut
  • ginger and garlic paste
  • two chilli's
  • 2 piece of onion
  • few curry leavers
  • 2 piece of tomato
  • pinch of salt(as of our needs)
  • garam masala
  • 250g of fresh chicken

Instruction

  1. Place the bowl in the stove and add few drops of oil to it
  2. After some time and add cinnamon leaves,geera and sombu and let it cook
  3. Add onions and tomato that are cut into small pieces
  4. Then after some time add ginger and garlic paste
  5. Cook all this for some time
  6. After some time add chicken and add water into into and let it boil for some time
  7. Then add coconut paste and kasakasa and wait for few minutes to cook
  8. Finally your gravy is ready

    9. Enjoy your food!!!
